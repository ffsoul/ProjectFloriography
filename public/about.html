ABOUT

Hyacinth Asparas chose to apply for a magnet school to get a fresh start in high school. Despite this, it seemed to her like it was just going to be the same as it's always been - another year of monotony.

As she was ready to accept her school life, a mysterious new student arrives to steal her heart.

"Let's discover what it means to love. Together."

SETTING

Project Floriography takes place in a country that could be America but not really. Expect Japanese rom-com tropes mixed with the life of a high school student in the 2020s.
